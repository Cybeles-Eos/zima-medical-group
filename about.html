Dr. Obinna Ozigbo

Internal Medicine and Primary Care Physician

Dr. Obinna Ozigbo is a Board-Certified Internal Medicine and Primary Care physician based in Helotes, Texas. He holds a Bachelor of Medical Science degree in Pharmacology and earned his medical degree with honors from the University of Port Harcourt, Nigeria. Dr. Ozigbo completed his Internal Medicine residency at New York Methodist Hospital in 2006 and has been dedicated to patient-centered care ever since. Known for his compassion and attention to detail, he takes time to listen and provide personalized treatment for every patient. He is a member of the American College of Physicians, Society of Hospital Medicine, and American Telemedicine Association, and is licensed to practice in Texas, Georgia, Virginia, and New Mexico.

Ijeoma Ozigbo is a board-certified Physician Assistant with over 20 years of clinical experience. Outside of her professional life, Ijeoma enjoys spending quality time with her family, embracing balance between her passion for medicine and her personal life.
